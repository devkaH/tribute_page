Oprah Winfrey, here giving a graduation speech at Agnes Scott College

Here's a time line of Oprah's life:

  • Born in Kosciusko, Mississippi on January 29 1954
  • She graduated from East Nashville High School as an honors student in 1971.
  • In 1971, Oprah was encouraged to enter the Miss Teen Fire Prevention pageant by a local radio disc jockey.
  • In 1976, Oprah moved to a TV station in Baltimore, Maryland.
  • In 19760 with the launch of The Oprah Winfrey Show, Oprah became one of the most famous people in the country.
  • Oprah was crowned Miss Black Tennessee in 1972.
  • As of 2013, her estimated net worth was $2.8 billion.
  • The Oprah Winfrey Show aired for 25 years. Its final episode was on May 24, 2011.
